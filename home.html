Welcome to Wordly

Wordly is a language-learning website that takes the time and hassle out of practicing a second language. With Wordly, it takes as little as 5 seconds to practice, making it ideal for learning on the go; whenever you have some time, open Wordly and strengthen your knowledge in Spanish, French or German.

From beginner to expert, Wordly allows you to customize your learning experience to your proficiency level. Wordly currently supports practice for nouns, verbs, and adjectives to increase your proficiency in the language of your choice.

Personalize your word feed by selecting the languages and proficiency levels you want to practice. Your Wordly Feed also allows you to strengthen your weaknesses by saving words to your word bank for later practice. And by logging into your Wordly account, you can access your word feed or word bank from any device.

Wordly gives you the power of quick, free, customizable, and hassle-free language learning at the touch of a button.

About Wordly

Wordly was created in 2018 by high school student James Weichert. is a language-learning website that takes the time and hassle out of practicing a second language. With Wordly, it takes as little as 5 seconds to practice, making it ideal for learning on the go; whenever you have some time, open Wordly and strengthen your knowledge in Spanish, French or German.

Currently, Wordly supports German, Spanish, and French with a database of over 3,000 nouns, verbs, and adjectives. Future updates will include an expanded library of words, support for phrases, and more languages like Dutch, Portugese, Italian, Arabic, Chinese, and Russian.
